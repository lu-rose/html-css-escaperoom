Bug Report 12 / 14

Feature Is Disabled

Open Reported by: Product Owner · Feature blocked from users

The feature is built and working — but it was accidentally shipped with a disabled attribute left on the button.

Final checkpoint

The button will light up once the attribute is removed.

In the Elements panel, find <button id="final-gate">. You will see a disabled attribute on it. Double-click the word disabled to select it, then press Delete — or right-click it and choose Delete attribute.
