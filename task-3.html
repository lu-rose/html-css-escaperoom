Bug Report 03 / 14

Button That Does Nothing

Open Reported by: Multiple users · Interaction blocked

Users are reporting that clicking the deploy button does nothing — even though it looks perfectly normal. Something in the code is silently blocking all interaction. Find what is in the way, remove it, and click the button to proceed.

Try clicking the button below.

It looks fine. So why doesn't it respond?
Find what is blocking it in DevTools and remove it.

In the Elements panel, look at the HTML around the button. There is a <div id="blocker"> sitting directly on top of it with a higher z-index. This transparent div intercepts every click before it reaches the button.

Select the blocker element, then press Delete on your keyboard — or right-click it and choose Delete element. Once removed, clicking the button will take you to the next task.
