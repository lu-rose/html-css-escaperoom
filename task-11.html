Text Is Hard to Read

Open Reported by: Accessibility audit · Readability issue

Users are struggling to read a piece of important information on this page. The text exists in the DOM, but something about its styling makes it nearly impossible to see. Fix it in DevTools to unlock the answer field, then enter what it says.

REVIEW

There is text in this box. Can you read it?

In the Elements panel, find <span id="tiny-text">. In the Styles pane, find font-size and change it from 0.5px to something like 32px. Read what the text says — that is your answer.
